Running

Running is a rapid movement characterised by a length of time where both feet are in the air. Walking is a slower form of movement in humans where one foot is on the ground at all times.

In the past, running allowed humans to be better hunters and escape from predators. Now, running is a mode of stress relief, boosting brain activity, and keeping healthy. It can be enjoyed in a group or independently; for example, many people enjoy Park Run, a group run in a park, in select locations across the UK.
